# Build a 10-company Singapore target list

**Sprint:** Sprint 3: Market Contact and Proof

**Lane:** Strait Up Growth

**Use this model or tool:** Gemini Deep Research or Perplexity for search. ChatGPT Project for qualification.

**Use this asset:** Strait Up Growth OS: audience-map.md, project qualification scorecard. Industry Authority Engine: relationship workflow.

## Starter prompt

```text
Help me build a target list of 10 Singapore founder-led B2B SMEs.

Criteria: 5 to 50 staff, service or tech-enabled business, visible founder, likely messy operations, potential CRM, sales process, AI workflow or growth systems need.

For each: company, founder or operator, why they fit, likely pain, opener angle, evidence source and bad-fit risk.

Constraint: no iGaming-first list, no generic startups, no companies without a clear reason.
```

## Human starter action

Use LinkedIn and Google. Pick 10. Do not over-research. Ten good-enough targets beats zero perfect targets.

## Expected output

Ten named Singapore prospects with pain hypothesis and opener angle.

## Success test

10 targets listed. 5 messages drafted.

## Friday review question

Did I speak to the market or stay in planning?

## Kill rule

Do not wait for the perfect ICP.
