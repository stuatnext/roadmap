# Build pricing and business vocabulary in Mandarin

**Sprint:** Sprint 2: Commercial Confidence

**Lane:** Chinese and Singapore

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT for phrase pack. Gemini for pronunciation. ChatGPT Tasks for reminder.

**Use this asset:** Business Mandarin note.

## Starter prompt

```text
Create a Business Mandarin phrase pack for discussing pricing, value, business growth and operations.

Include:
1. 20 words.
2. 10 phrases.
3. One short roleplay with a founder.
4. Pinyin and English.
5. A 15-minute drill.

Keep it practical for Singapore business conversations.
```

## Human starter action

Practise introducing one commercial concept in Mandarin.

## Expected output

A short Mandarin phrase pack around price, value, growth, systems and business problems.

## Success test

One roleplay completed.

## Friday review question

Can I explain one business idea more confidently in Mandarin?

## Kill rule

Do not chase fluency. Chase useful phrases.
