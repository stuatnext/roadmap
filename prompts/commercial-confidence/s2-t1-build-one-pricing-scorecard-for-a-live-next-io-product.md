# Build one pricing scorecard for a live NEXT.io product

**Sprint:** Sprint 2: Commercial Confidence

**Lane:** Commercial Confidence

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT Project first. DeepSeek after for red-team critique. Perplexity or Deep Research only for current competitor evidence.

**Use this asset:** Commercial Director Advisory Board: pricing scorecard, sponsorship packaging scorecard, media monetisation scorecard

## Starter prompt

```text
Context: I need to defend pricing at NEXT.io with more rigour and less gut feel.

Task: Score one product for pricing confidence. Ask me for missing data before recommending.

Criteria: buyer value, audience quality, ROI proof, scarcity, delivery effort, margin, sales difficulty, discount risk, strategic value.

Output: scorecard, strongest argument, weakest assumption, data gap, recommendation: keep price, raise price, lower price or repackage.

Constraint: do not pretend missing data exists. Mark assumptions clearly.

Success test: I can defend the pricing view in a meeting.
```

## Human starter action

Pick one product. Write current price, deliverables, buyer, capacity and known sales objections. Then run the prompt.

## Expected output

One product has a defensible pricing view based on value, scarcity, demand, margin and delivery risk.

## Success test

One scorecard completed. One data gap identified.

## Friday review question

What pricing assumption did I test instead of guessing?

## Kill rule

Do not let AI benchmark competitors without current sources.
