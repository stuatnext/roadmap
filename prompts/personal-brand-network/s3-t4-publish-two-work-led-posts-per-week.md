# Publish two work-led posts per week

**Sprint:** Sprint 3: Market Contact and Proof

**Lane:** Personal Brand

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT Project for extraction. Claude for tone. Human final.

**Use this asset:** Industry Authority Engine: work-led post template, confidentiality filter, recurring obsessions, authority scorecard.

## Starter prompt

```text
Ask me 5 questions to extract a LinkedIn post from my week.

Focus on: what annoyed me, what I fixed, what most businesses get wrong, what AI failed at, what I would tell a founder.

Then draft 2 posts under 160 words. Voice: blunt, useful, human. No NEXT.io details. No corporate fluff. No AI slop.
```

## Human starter action

Write one rough paragraph yourself first. Use AI to sharpen, not replace your thinking.

## Expected output

Two safe, useful posts per week that turn private frustration into public authority.

## Success test

Two posts published or scheduled. No confidential details.

## Friday review question

What did I make visible this week?

## Kill rule

If it sounds like an AI influencer, bin it.
