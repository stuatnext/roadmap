# Run the weekly content extraction drill

**Sprint:** Sprint 5: Authority and Network

**Lane:** Authority

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT Project for extraction. Claude for voice. Human final.

**Use this asset:** Industry Authority Engine: confidentiality filter, work-led post template, authority scorecard.

## Starter prompt

```text
Interview me for 7 minutes to extract content from my week.

Ask only one question at a time.

Extract:
1. one thing that annoyed me
2. one thing I fixed
3. one thing businesses get wrong
4. one AI failure
5. one leadership lesson
6. one Singapore or founder lesson
7. one thing I would tell a client

Then produce:
2 LinkedIn posts under 160 words
3 useful comment angles
1 relationship follow-up message

Rules: no NEXT.io details, no cringe, no AI slop, no generic advice.
```

## Human starter action

Answer the interview in rough bullets. Do not polish before extraction.

## Expected output

Two posts, three comment angles and one relationship follow-up idea per week.

## Success test

Two posts drafted, one published.

## Friday review question

What public signal did I send this week?

## Kill rule

If the posts sound safe and dull, rewrite from the annoyance.
