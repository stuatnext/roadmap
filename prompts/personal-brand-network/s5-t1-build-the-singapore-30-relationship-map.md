# Build the Singapore 30 relationship map

**Sprint:** Sprint 5: Authority and Network

**Lane:** Network

**Use this model or tool:** Gemini Deep Research or Perplexity for discovery. ChatGPT Project for mapping. Human judgement for final list.

**Use this asset:** Industry Authority Engine: relationship follow-up workflow. Strait Up Growth OS: audience map.

## Starter prompt

```text
Build a Singapore relationship map for me.

Groups:
1. potential buyers
2. mentors
3. connectors
4. founders and operators
5. AI and tech builders
6. speaking or media opportunities

For each person: name, role, company, why they matter, value I can offer, opener angle, risk of wasting time, first action.

Constraint: quality over fame. Avoid generic influencer lists.
```

## Human starter action

Start with 10 names from LinkedIn, events, Tim, Vincent and existing Singapore contacts. Then expand.

## Expected output

30 people grouped by buyer, mentor, connector, peer and opportunity source.

## Success test

30 people mapped. 5 first actions chosen.

## Friday review question

Did I build a relationship pipeline or just collect names?

## Kill rule

If there is no reason to contact them, remove them.
