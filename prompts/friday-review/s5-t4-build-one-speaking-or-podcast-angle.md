# Build one speaking or podcast angle

**Sprint:** Sprint 5: Authority and Network

**Lane:** Opportunities

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT Project for topic strategy. Claude for pitch writing. Perplexity for target shows and events.

**Use this asset:** Industry Authority Engine: speaking-topic ideas, podcast guest angles, expert-network positioning.

## Starter prompt

```text
Create one speaking or podcast pitch based on my positioning.

Positioning: blunt growth operator helping founder-led B2B businesses fix operational drag, CRM mess, AI confusion and weak ownership.

Output:
1. topic title
2. 100-word abstract
3. why I am credible
4. three talking points
5. audience fit
6. five target podcasts or events
7. outreach message

Constraints: no generic AI speaker nonsense. It must sound useful and commercially grounded.
```

## Human starter action

Pick one topic you would be willing to talk about without a script.

## Expected output

One tight topic, pitch and target list.

## Success test

One pitch created. Three targets identified.

## Friday review question

Did I create an opportunity asset?

## Kill rule

If the topic sounds like everyone else, sharpen the opinion.
