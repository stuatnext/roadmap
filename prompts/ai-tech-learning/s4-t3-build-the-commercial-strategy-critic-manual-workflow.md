# Build the Commercial Strategy Critic manual workflow

**Sprint:** Sprint 4: Manual AI Workflows

**Lane:** AI Leverage

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT Project for primary critique. DeepSeek for second opinion. Claude for stakeholder adaptation.

**Use this asset:** Commercial Director Advisory Board: scorecards, output templates. LLM Control Hub: red team review.

## Starter prompt

```text
Act as my Commercial Strategy Critic.

First ask for missing data. Then review this strategy or decision for:
1. revenue logic
2. pricing defensibility
3. missing data
4. assumptions
5. sales reality
6. operational dependencies
7. delivery risk
8. CRM tracking gaps
9. discount policy risks
10. stakeholder objections

Output:
Recommendation, confidence score, strongest point, weakest point, missing data, questions for Will, questions for Ana, questions for Pierre, next action.

Do not make up competitor facts. Mark uncertainty.
```

## Human starter action

Use it on one live NEXT.io strategy or pricing item. Do not use it for theoretical exercises.

## Expected output

A reusable critic prompt for pricing, GTM, targets and strategy.

## Success test

One strategy improves before a meeting.

## Friday review question

What did AI catch that I would have missed?

## Kill rule

If it gives generic strategy advice, fail it through the Quality Gate.
