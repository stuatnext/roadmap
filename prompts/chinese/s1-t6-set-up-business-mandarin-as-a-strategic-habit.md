# Set up Business Mandarin as a strategic habit

**Sprint:** Sprint 1: Relief and Control

**Lane:** Chinese and Singapore

**Use this model or tool:** ChatGPT for scripts. Gemini for pronunciation help. NotebookLM if turning your own scripts into study material. ChatGPT Tasks for reminders.

**Use this asset:** Create new asset: Business Mandarin note. No mature asset exists yet.

## Starter prompt

```text
Context: I am moving to Singapore and building Strait Up Growth. Chinese is a strategic relationship asset, not a hobby.

Task: Build my first Business Mandarin practice pack.

Output:
1. 10 business words.
2. 5 practical phrases.
3. 60-second self-introduction.
4. Pinyin and English.
5. 10-minute practice routine.

Topics: growth, operations, CRM, sales process, AI, efficiency, Singapore, founder-led businesses.

Constraint: practical business language only. No school textbook fluff.
```

## Human starter action

Create a note with five headings: intro, Strait Up Growth, business problems, follow-up, small talk. Practise 15 minutes.

## Expected output

A working Business Mandarin note and four 15-minute practice blocks per week.

## Success test

Four short sessions completed this sprint.

## Friday review question

Did Chinese become a working asset, not a vague ambition?

## Kill rule

If you start researching the perfect language app for an hour, stop and practise one script.
